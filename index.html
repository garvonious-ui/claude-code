Questions? We've got answers.

Our kids mats are designed for ages 3–12. They're proportioned specifically for smaller bodies—not just shrunken adult mats. The dimensions, thickness, and weight are all optimized for children's comfort and safety during practice.

Premium natural rubber base with a microfiber suede top. 5mm thick for optimal cushioning and stability. We use non-toxic, eco-friendly inks for all designs. Absolutely no PVC, phthalates, or heavy metals. Safe enough for the youngest yogis.

10% of net revenue from every sale goes directly to species-specific conservation organizations. Buy a Tiger mat, and you fund WWF Tigers Alive. Buy a Sea Turtle mat, and you fund ocean habitat protection. Every mat includes a Conservation Impact Card that explains exactly which organization receives the donation and how it helps.

Yes! Our Parent + Child Bundle lets you pick the same species in both adult and kids sizes. Same beautiful design, proportioned for each body. We also offer Sibling Packs and Family Packs for families who want to practice together.
