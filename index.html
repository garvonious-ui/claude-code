More Than a Mat

Wild Form Yoga was born from a simple belief: our yoga practice can extend beyond the mat to make a real difference in the world. Each of our mats is shaped like an endangered animal, serving as a daily reminder of the beautiful creatures that share our planet—and our responsibility to protect them.
