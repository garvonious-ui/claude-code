Our Story

Where yoga meets wildlife conservation

Why Wild Form?

Wild Form Yoga began with a moment of clarity during a morning practice. Watching the sunrise over a wildlife sanctuary, our founder realized that the same mindfulness we cultivate on our mats could extend to how we care for our planet and its incredible creatures.

We asked ourselves: what if every sun salutation could contribute to saving the species that inspire awe and wonder? What if the shape of our mat could remind us daily of our connection to the natural world?

That question became Wild Form Yogaâ€”a brand where every product is designed with purpose, every purchase makes an impact, and every practice becomes an act of conservation.

Our Conservation Commitment

10% of every sale donated to wildlife conservation

We partner with established conservation organizations working on the ground to protect endangered species and their habitats. From anti-poaching initiatives to habitat restoration, your purchase directly funds the fight to preserve our planet's biodiversity.

The Animals We Honor

Each design represents an endangered species and the urgent need to protect them

Bengal Tiger

Endangered

Fewer than 4,500 remain in the wild. Habitat loss and poaching threaten their survival. Our Tiger Mat honors their strength and grace while supporting protection efforts.

Giant Panda

Vulnerable

Conservation efforts have helped pandas recover, but only about 1,800 remain wild. The Panda Mat celebrates this success story while supporting continued protection.

Mountain Gorilla

Endangered

With around 1,000 individuals left, mountain gorillas face threats from habitat destruction and disease. Our Gorilla Mat supports their forest homes.
